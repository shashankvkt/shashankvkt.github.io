I am a Master's student majoring in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida, USA. Prior joing UCF, I was a Junior Research Fellow in the Image Analysis and Computer Vision Lab at the Indian Institute of Science, India under the guidance of Dr. Soma Biswas. I completed my Bachelors in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Mumbai in Summer 2017.

I am fascinated by technology which can help us lead better lives in numerous ways. I love to work on projects which are challenging and difficult yet interesting and meaningful. My career goal is to pursue mastery in this field and apply the knowledge for solving problems of our society. I am looking for Computer Vision / Deep Learning internships for Summer 2019.
